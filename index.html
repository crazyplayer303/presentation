What are Cloud Governance Policies?

Policies, procedures, and controls are the foundation of cloud governance that manage the deployment, use, and security of cloud services within an organization.

What is Cloud Compliance & Risk Management?

The structured approach to managing risks and ensuring cloud operations meet regulatory requirements and industry standards.

üîç Risk Assessment Process:
